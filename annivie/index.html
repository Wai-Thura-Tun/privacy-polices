Annivie Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 25, 2025

Welcome to Annivie, an app designed to help you track and celebrate important dates. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect your information when you use Annivie. By using the app, you agree to this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use Annivie.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information:
    • Name: Optionally collected when you sign in with Google or Apple.
    • Email Address: Optionally collected during sign-in for account creation and synchronization.
    • User ID: Unique identifier generated by Firebase for signed-in users.
    • User Content: Anniversary details (names, dates, notes) and timezone (from device settings) you provide to track events.
    • Photos or Videos: Images you upload or capture via camera for anniversary participants.
  • Device Information:
    • Device ID: Apple Push Notification service (APNs) token for reminders and an advertising ID (IDFA) for ads if tracking is enabled.
    • Coarse Location: Approximate location data (not precise GPS) collected by AdMob for personalized ads.
  • Usage Data:
    • Product Interaction: Anonymized app usage events (e.g., button clicks) collected by Firebase Analytics.
    • Advertising Data: Ad impressions and clicks collected by Google AdMob if tracking is allowed.

2. How We Collect Information

We collect information through:

  • User Input: Name, email, anniversary details, photos/videos, and timezone you provide.
  • Automatic Collection: Device ID and usage data when you use the app; coarse location and ad data when ads are shown.
  • Third-Party Services:
    • Google and Apple Sign-In for name, email, and User ID.
    • Firebase for Device ID, timezone, and analytics.
    • Google AdMob for advertising data.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide App Functionality:
    • Store anniversary details and photos locally (Realm) or synced (Firestore) for signed-in users.
    • Send push notifications using Device ID and timezone via Firebase.
  • Analytics: Analyze anonymized usage data (Firebase Analytics) to improve Annivie.
  • Advertising: Deliver personalized ads via Google AdMob using Device ID and coarse location if you opt in.

4. How We Share Your Information

We share your information with:

  • Firebase (Google): Name, email, User ID, Device ID, timezone, and photos for authentication, storage, and notifications.
  • Google AdMob: Device ID, coarse location, and ad data for personalized ads (if tracking enabled).
  • Legal Obligations: If required by law or to protect our rights.

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

5. Data Storage and Security

Your information is stored:

  • Locally: Anniversary details and photos in Realm on your device (unsynced for guests).
  • In the Cloud: Name, email, User ID, Device ID, timezone, and synced data in Firebase Firestore, encrypted in transit and at rest.
  • Security Measures: Industry-standard encryption protects your data, though no system is 100% secure.

6. Your Choices and Rights

You can:

  • Use as Guest: Avoid syncing personal data.
  • Ad Tracking: Opt out via iOS App Tracking Transparency (disables IDFA).
  • Data Access/Deletion: Enable to access or delete your data.
  • Notifications: Disable in device settings to stop Device ID use.

GDPR/CCPA Rights: EU/CA users can request data access, deletion, or restriction. Email us with your request, and we’ll respond within 30 days.

7. Third-Party Services

We use:

  • Google (Sign-In, Firebase, AdMob)
  • Apple (Sign-In)

8. Children’s Privacy

Annivie is not for kids under 13. We don’t knowingly collect their data—contact us if we have.
