Annivie Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 25, 2025

Welcome to Annivie, an app designed to help you track and celebrate important dates. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect your information when you use Annivie. By using the app, you agree to this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use Annivie.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information:
    • Name: Optionally collected when you sign in with Google or Apple.
    • Email Address: Optionally collected during sign-in for account creation and synchronization.
    • User Content: Anniversary details (names, dates, notes) you enter to track events.
    • Photos: Images you upload for anniversary participants (stored locally and synced for signed-in users).
  • Device Information:
    • Device ID: An Apple Push Notification service (APNs) token to send you reminders and notifications.
    • Precise Location: Your timezone, derived from device settings to schedule notifications (not GPS coordinates).
  • Ad-Related Information:
    • Advertising Data: Device information (e.g., IP address, advertising ID) collected by Google AdMob to deliver personalized ads if you allow tracking.

2. How We Collect Information

We collect information through:

  • User Input: Name, email, anniversary details, and photos you provide via the app.
  • Automatic Collection: Device ID and timezone when you enable notifications; ad data when ads are shown.
  • Third-Party Services:
    • Google and Apple Sign-In for name and email.
    • Firebase for Device ID and timezone.
    • Google AdMob for advertising data.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide App Functionality:
    • Store anniversary details and photos using Realm and Firestore.
    • Sync data for signed-in users via Firebase Firestore.
    • Send push notifications using your Device ID and timezone via Firebase.
  • Deliver Personalized Ads: Google AdMob uses device data for relevant ads if you opt in via iOS tracking permission.
  • Improve the App: Anonymized usage data to enhance features (no personal data used for analytics).

4. How We Share Your Information

We share your information with:

  • Firebase (Google): Device ID and timezone for notification services.
  • Google AdMob: Device data for ad personalization (if tracking is enabled).
  • Legal Obligations: If required by law, to comply with legal processes, or to protect our rights.

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

5. Data Storage and Security

Your information is stored:

  • Locally: Anniversary details and photos in Realm on your device.
  • In the Cloud: Name, email, Device ID, timezone, and synced data in Firebase Firestore, encrypted in transit and at rest.
  • Security Measures: We use industry-standard encryption to protect your data, though no system is completely secure.

6. Your Choices and Rights

You have the following options:

  • Account Options: Use Annivie as a guest to avoid syncing personal data.
  • Ad Tracking: Opt out of personalized ads via the iOS App Tracking Transparency prompt.
  • Data Rights: Request access to, correction of, or deletion of your data by contacting us (see below).
  • Notification Settings: Disable notifications in your device settings to stop Device ID collection.

For users in the EU (GDPR) or California (CCPA), you have additional rights to access, delete, or restrict processing of your data.
